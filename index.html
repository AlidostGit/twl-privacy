Privacy Policy

The Waste Lab Driver App  ·  Last updated June 2026
The TWL Driver App is an internal operational tool available exclusively to authorised employees and contractors of The Waste Lab Recycling Consultancy LLC. This policy explains how we handle data collected during shift operations.
1

Introduction

The Waste Lab Driver App is operated by The Waste Lab Recycling Consultancy LLC, registered in the United Arab Emirates. The App is not intended for use by the general public.

2

Information We Collect

📍 Location Data
GPS access to record stop arrivals and support route management. Active during shift sessions only.
📷 Photos & Camera
Site inspections, vehicle condition, skip images, odometer readings, and evidence photos.
📋 Shift & Operational Data
Stop arrivals, skip counts, waste weights, odometer readings, and inspection results.
👤 Personal Identifiers
Driver name and employee ID associated with shift records for operational accountability.
3

How We Use Your Information

Data collected is used to record and verify food waste collection operations, enable data verification by our data team, support operational reporting and client billing, ensure driver and site safety compliance, and improve app performance and reliability.

4

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on The Waste Lab's secure servers hosted on DigitalOcean infrastructure. Photos are stored in private cloud object storage. Access is restricted to authorised The Waste Lab personnel only. We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

5

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent personal data to third parties. Data may be shared internally with The Waste Lab operations and data teams. Data may be disclosed to regulatory authorities if required by UAE law.

6

Data Retention

Operational data and photos are retained for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this policy or as required by applicable law.

7

Your Rights

As an authorised employee or contractor of The Waste Lab Recycling Consultancy LLC, you may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us using the details below.

8

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated through official The Waste Lab channels.
